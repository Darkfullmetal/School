General game tips

  • Use windower or Ashita as the plugins help with QOL.
  • Pick a support job that complements your playstyle and use it to help gear up other jobs.
  • Inventory space will be your endgame...
  • Get all the story quests done. There are things that are story locked for content.
  • Don't be afraid to ask for help or advice from other players.
