Welcome to Disturbed's Official Website

Disturbed is an American heavy metal band from Chicago, formed in 1994. The band consists of vocalist David Draiman, guitarist Dan Donegan, bassist John Moyer, and drummer Mike Wengren. Disturbed is known for their powerful sound and energetic performances, with hit albums like The Sickness and Indestructible. They have sold over 17 million records worldwide and are recognized for songs such as "Down with the Sickness," "Stricken," and their cover of "The Sound of Silence."
